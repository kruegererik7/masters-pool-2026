SCORING RULES
  • Thursday & Friday: All 5 players' scores count toward your daily total.
  • Saturday & Sunday: Only your top 4 scores count (best 4 of your active players).
  • Penalty: If 2 or more of your players miss the cut, your score for each weekend day equals the worst individual score in the field that day.
  • Winner: Lowest cumulative combined score across all four rounds.
